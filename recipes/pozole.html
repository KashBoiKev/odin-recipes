Pozole

Description

Pozole is traditional, hearty Mexican soup or stew made from hominy (dried maize kernels) and meat, typically simmered in a rich, flavorful broth. It is known for its chewy corn texture and is highly customizable, usually served with a variety of fresh garnishes like shredded cabbage, radish, lime, and cilantro.
